What is the Knowledge Hub?

The Knowledge Hub is a reference library for the AI coach. When a learner asks a topic question, the coach searches this library first to enrich its answer with accurate, context-specific information from your own documents — e.g. study notes, textbooks, SOPs, or course PDFs.

What to upload: Plain-text files (.txt), Markdown (.md), CSV summaries, or copy-pasted notes via the manual entry form. Avoid uploading binary or encrypted PDFs — use a PDF-to-text converter first.
